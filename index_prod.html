Features

At Swaraag Studio We believe Music is a Feel which is Endless to an Endless and A Place Where We Come Alive. We are ready to get involved at any stage of your music production process, from concept and consultancy to post-production. We are aligned to your artistic, technical and business needs.

Music Creation and Compositions

We understand that finding the right music can be a tricky task. In order to get the perfect tracks, our musical scores are created from scratch, giving your song, film or video production an extra dynamic element which is unsurpassed in terms of quality and relevance.

Music Recording and Arrangements

Record your music in an environment designed by musicians, for musicians. With great equipment, acoustics, tasteful visual design and professional engineers, Swaraag Studio is a place not just for capturing music, but for creating it.

Audio Design, Mixing and Editing

From complex musical briefs, sound design, Automated Dialogue Replacement, all the way to the final mix, we make sure the audio for your requirement is bang-on target.



Dubbing and Voice Overs

Every project is treated with a fresh perspective to create a unique product, with FX, background scores, character voices, voice overs, build ups - we’ll give you every imaginable sound you need for your concept.

Foley and Sound Effects

Music, dialogues, sound effects & Foley. It’s not just about laying down sounds but creating a sound scape that supports the story and gives an identity to your idea.

Song Writing and Lyrics

To provide that extra bit of personal assistance, Swaraag Studio can even offer song writing & lyrical consultancy and advisory service to artists, filmmakers and producers.
